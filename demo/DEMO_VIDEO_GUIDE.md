# Demo Video Guide

Recommended length: 3 to 5 minutes.

## Recording Tools

You can use:

- Zoom screen recording;
- QuickTime Player on macOS;
- Loom;
- OBS Studio.

## Suggested Script

### 1. Introduction

"This is an acute malnutrition early warning dashboard for district-level monitoring and forecasting."

Show the app title and sidebar.

### 2. Load Data

Show the CSV path or upload a CSV. Explain the dataset contains monthly district health, climate, and population indicators.

### 3. Spearman Correlation

Show the radial Spearman correlation portrait. Explain that it identifies covariates associated with acute malnutrition.

### 4. Compare Districts and Regions

Select several districts and compare them against the national reference. Then switch to regions.

### 5. Historical Risk Trend

Show the stacked Normal, Alert, Alarm, and Emergency historical trend chart. Explain the 6-month Emergency trend line.

### 6. Forecasts

Open the Forecasts tab. Show the national forecast and a single district forecast.

### 7. Maps

Upload/select the GeoJSON district name column. Show current and forecast risk maps.

### 8. Model Evaluation

Open Model Evaluation. Highlight MAE, RMSE, sensitivity, balanced accuracy, and macro recall.

### 9. Closing

"The tool helps identify districts that may need closer monitoring, targeted nutrition response, or further field validation."
